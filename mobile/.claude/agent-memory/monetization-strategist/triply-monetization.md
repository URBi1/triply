# Triply — Full Monetization Analysis (2026-03-16)

## 7 Revenue Streams — Detailed

### 1. Freemium Subscription (PRIMARY)
- Free: 3 trips, 200 photos/trip, 90-day storage
- Pro $4.99/mo or $39.99/yr: unlimited trips, 1000 photos/trip, unlimited storage, HD quality
- Potential: with 10k MAU and 5% conversion = $2,495 MRR
- Complexity: Medium (RevenueCat + paywall screen)

### 2. Physical Prints / Photo Books (QUICK WIN)
- Integration with Printful or Gelato print-on-demand
- Photo book from $19.99, prints from $4.99
- 30-40% margin after fulfillment
- Complexity: Medium-High (layout editor needed)

### 3. Storage Expansion (One-time purchases)
- +500 photos pack: $1.99, +5GB storage: $2.99
- Low friction, impulse buy at upload limit
- Complexity: Low

### 4. Album Themes / UI Customization
- Premium themes, cover styles, grid layouts: $0.99-$2.99 one-time or included in Pro
- Low complexity, high perceived value for sharing

### 5. AI Features (future)
- Auto-captions, highlight reels, face grouping, map visualization
- Bundle into Pro or premium add-on $2.99/mo
- Complexity: High

### 6. Travel Partner Integrations / Affiliate
- Booking.com, GetYourGuide affiliate links contextual to trip location
- ~3-8% commission on bookings
- Complexity: Low (affiliate links only)

### 7. B2B / White-label (long-term)
- Travel agencies, wedding photographers, tour operators
- $29-99/mo per account
- Complexity: High, post-PMF only
